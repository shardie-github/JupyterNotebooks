# Agent Factory Platform - Education Focus

## Partnership with McGraw Hill Education

Agent Factory Platform is strategically designed for **higher education institutions** and **lifelong learning organizations** in partnership with McGraw Hill Education.

### Our Mission

Empower educational institutions to create, deploy, and scale AI-powered learning agents that enhance student engagement, support personalized learning, and improve educational outcomes.

## Target Audience

### Primary Users
- **Higher Education Institutions**: Universities, colleges, and community colleges
- **Lifelong Learning Organizations**: Professional development providers, continuing education programs
- **Educational Technology Teams**: IT departments, instructional design teams
- **Faculty & Instructors**: Professors, teaching assistants, course designers

### Lead Sources
- Partnership inquiries via [McGraw Hill Education Partnerships](https://www.mheducation.ca/partnerships)
- Word of mouth referrals from existing educational partners
- Direct inquiries from educational institutions

## Education-Focused Features

### Institutional Multi-Tenancy

Each educational institution gets:
- **Isolated environment**: Complete data separation
- **Custom branding**: Institution-specific branding
- **Resource quotas**: Based on institution size
- **Usage analytics**: Track adoption and engagement

### FERPA Compliance

- **Data privacy**: Student data protection
- **Access controls**: Role-based permissions
- **Audit trails**: Complete activity logging
- **Data retention**: Configurable retention policies

### Integration with Learning Management Systems

- **LMS Integration**: Canvas, Blackboard, Moodle support
- **Single Sign-On (SSO)**: SAML, OAuth integration
- **Gradebook Sync**: Automated grade updates
- **Roster Management**: Student enrollment sync

### Accessibility

- **WCAG 2.1 AA Compliance**: Accessible interfaces
- **Screen reader support**: Full compatibility
- **Keyboard navigation**: Complete keyboard access
- **Multi-language support**: Internationalization ready
