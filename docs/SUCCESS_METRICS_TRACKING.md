## TRACKING METHODOLOGY

### Weekly Tracking

**Frequency**: Every Friday  
**Format**: Update status column in metrics tables  
**Review**: Weekly standup meeting  
**Action**: Address any red/yellow status items

### Monthly Review

**Frequency**: End of each month  
**Format**: Comprehensive metrics review  
**Review**: Monthly business review meeting  
**Action**: Adjust plan if metrics off-track

### Phase Completion Review

**Frequency**: End of each phase (Weeks 6, 12, 18, 24)  
**Format**: Phase retrospective + metrics review  
**Review**: Phase completion meeting  
**Action**: Sign off on phase completion, plan next phase

## METRICS DASHBOARD

### Recommended Tools

1. **Project Management**: Asana/Jira (track deliverables)
2. **Spreadsheet**: Google Sheets (metrics tracking)
3. **Analytics**: Mixpanel/Amplitude (product metrics)
4. **BI Tool**: Tableau/Looker (business metrics)

### Dashboard Structure

1. **Phase Progress**: Overall phase completion %
2. **Deliverable Status**: Green/Yellow/Red status
3. **Budget Tracking**: Actual vs planned spend
4. **Timeline Tracking**: On-time vs delayed
5. **Quality Metrics**: Review completion, test results

---

## RISK INDICATORS

### Early Warning Signs

1. **Timeline Risk**: > 2 weeks behind schedule
2. **Budget Risk**: > 10% over budget
3. **Quality Risk**: > 3 deliverables rejected
4. **Resource Risk**: Key consultant unavailable
5. **Scope Risk**: > 5 new requirements added

### Mitigation Actions

1. **Timeline Risk**: Re-prioritize, add resources, reduce scope
2. **Budget Risk**: Review spend, optimize costs, re-allocate
3. **Quality Risk**: Increase review cycles, add quality checkpoints
4. **Resource Risk**: Identify backups, start early outreach
5. **Scope Risk**: Freeze scope, defer to next phase

---

## SUCCESS DEFINITION

### Phase Success

**Green**: All metrics meet targets, on-time, on-budget  
**Yellow**: Most metrics meet targets, minor delays/issues  
**Red**: Significant gaps, delays, or budget overruns

### Overall Success

**Successful**: 
- ✅ All phases completed
- ✅ < 5% gaps remaining
- ✅ On-time completion (within 2 weeks)
- ✅ On-budget (within 10%)
- ✅ Quality standards met

**Needs Improvement**:
- ⚠️ Some phases incomplete
- ⚠️ 5-10% gaps remaining
- ⚠️ Delayed completion (2-4 weeks)
- ⚠️ Budget overrun (10-20%)

**At Risk**:
- ❌ Significant phases incomplete
- ❌ > 10% gaps remaining
- ❌ Delayed completion (> 4 weeks)
- ❌ Budget overrun (> 20%)
