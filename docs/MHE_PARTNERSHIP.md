# McGraw Hill Education Partnership

## Strategic Partnership Overview

Agent Factory Platform is designed in strategic partnership with **McGraw Hill Education** to serve higher education institutions and lifelong learning organizations.

## Partnership Model

### Lead Generation

Partnership inquiries come from:
1. **Partnership Form**: [www.mheducation.ca/partnerships](https://www.mheducation.ca/partnerships)
2. **Word of Mouth**: Referrals from existing educational partners
3. **Direct Inquiries**: Educational institutions reaching out directly

### Target Institutions

- **Higher Education**: Universities, colleges, community colleges
- **Lifelong Learning**: Professional development, continuing education
- **Educational Organizations**: Non-profits, training providers

## Partnership Benefits

### For McGraw Hill Education

1. **Enhanced Offerings**: AI-powered educational solutions

## Partnership Process

### Step 1: Initial Inquiry

Institutions can reach out via:
- **Partnership Portal**: [www.mheducation.ca/partnerships](https://www.mheducation.ca/partnerships)
- **Email**: partnerships@mheducation.ca
- **Referral**: Word of mouth from existing partners

### Step 2: Needs Assessment

- **Discovery Call**: Understand institutional needs
- **Use Case Identification**: Identify specific use cases
- **Technical Review**: Assess technical requirements
- **Pilot Planning**: Plan initial pilot program

### Step 3: Pilot Program

- **Setup**: Configure institutional tenant
- **Deployment**: Deploy initial agents/workflows
- **Training**: Train faculty and staff
- **Evaluation**: Measure success metrics

### Step 4: Full Implementation

- **Scale**: Expand to additional courses/programs
- **Optimization**: Refine based on feedback
- **Support**: Ongoing partnership support
