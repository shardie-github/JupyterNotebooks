# Agent Factory Platform - Business Case

## Executive Summary

**Agent Factory Platform** is a production-ready platform for building, deploying, and monetizing AI agents, strategically designed for higher education institutions and lifelong learning organizations in partnership with McGraw Hill Education.

**Investment Required**: $500K - $1M  
**Timeline to Break-Even**: 18-24 months  
**5-Year Revenue Projection**: $10M+ ARR  
**Market Opportunity**: $50B+ (AI agent market)

---

## 1. Problem Statement

### Current State
- Building production-ready AI agents requires 3-6 months and $50K-$200K in engineering costs
- Educational institutions lack AI engineering resources to build custom solutions
- Existing platforms are either too complex (developer-focused) or too limited (no-code)
- No marketplace exists for reusable, monetizable agent components

### Pain Points
1. **For Developers**: Building agents from scratch is time-consuming and expensive
2. **For Educational Institutions**: Lack of AI expertise and resources
3. **For Founders**: Can't afford $200K+ engineering teams
4. **For Enterprises**: Need compliance, security, and scalability

---

## 2. Solution Overview

**Agent Factory Platform** provides:
- **Composable Library**: Python library for building agents quickly
- **Blueprint Marketplace**: Pre-built, monetizable agent bundles
- **Production Infrastructure**: Built-in security, compliance, scaling
- **Education Focus**: Tailored for higher education institutions

**Key Differentiators**:
1. Education-first design (FERPA compliant, LMS integration)
2. McGraw Hill Education partnership (distribution channel)
3. Marketplace flywheel (network effects)
4. Production-ready from day one

---

## 4. Business Model

### Revenue Streams

1. **SaaS Subscriptions** (60% of revenue)
   - Starter: $49/month
   - Pro: $199/month
   - Enterprise: $999-$10K/month

2. **Marketplace Commissions** (20% of revenue)
   - 30% commission on Blueprint sales
   - Revenue share with creators

3. **Enterprise Licenses** (15% of revenue)
   - Custom pricing: $50K-$500K/year
   - On-premise deployments

4. **Professional Services** (5% of revenue)
   - Custom development
   - Training and consulting

### Education-Specific Revenue Model
- **Per-Institution Licensing**: $10K-$100K/year
- **Per-Student Pricing**: $5-$20/student/year
- **Partnership Revenue Share**: 70/30 split with McGraw Hill
