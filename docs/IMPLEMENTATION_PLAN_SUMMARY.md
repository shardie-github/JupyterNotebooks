## Phase Summary

### Phase 1: Foundation & Critical Gaps (Weeks 1-6)
**Focus**: Business foundation, legal compliance, market intelligence  
**Budget**: $46,000 (consultants) + $15,000 (team) = $61,000  
**Key Deliverables**:
- Business case document
- Financial model (5-year)
- Legal documents (ToS, Privacy Policy, etc.)
- Compliance documentation (GDPR, FERPA, SOC2)
- Market analysis
- Competitive analysis

**Success Criteria**: All critical gaps addressed, legal approved, market validated

---

### Phase 2: Sales & Marketing Foundation (Weeks 7-12)
**Focus**: Sales enablement, marketing infrastructure, customer success  
**Budget**: $46,000 (consultants) + $25,000 (team) = $71,000  
**Key Deliverables**:
- Sales process and materials
- Marketing plan and materials
- Customer success framework
- Support infrastructure

**Success Criteria**: Sales team enabled, marketing ready, support operational

---

### Phase 3: Operations & Product (Weeks 13-18)
**Focus**: Operational excellence, product roadmap, technical documentation  
**Budget**: $41,000 (consultants) + $20,000 (team) = $61,000  
**Key Deliverables**:

- Partnership agreements
- Product roadmap (12 months)
- Technical documentation
- Integration guides

**Success Criteria**: Operations ready for scale, roadmap approved, docs complete

### Phase 4: Training & Analytics (Weeks 19-24)

## Risk Mitigation

### Key Risks
1. **Resource Constraints**: Prioritize critical items, use consultants strategically
2. **Timeline Delays**: Buffer time built in, flexible prioritization
3. **Market Changes**: Regular market review, agile adjustments
4. **Partnership Dependencies**: Regular communication, flexible planning
5. **Quality Issues**: Review processes, quality checkpoints

### Mitigation Strategies
- Weekly standups and reviews
- Monthly phase completion reviews
- Early risk identification
- Contingency planning
- Stakeholder alignment
